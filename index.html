The Egyptian problem we chose

The pressing Egyptian issue we picked is the prevalence of trash-filled streets. This not only contributes significantly to pollution and the broader issue of global warming but also deters tourists, affecting a crucial component of our economy. A cleaner environment would not only be better for the planet but could also enhance our tourism sector significantly. Overall we believe that this problem is a crucial one that we can’t keep ignoring because if we do then we will ruin our country and its beauty.


Why we chose this idea

Our team was motivated by the amount of trash and litter that fills the streets of Egypt. Not only does the amount of pollution affect our country’s beauty but also affect the health of our community and our economy. This issue if not solved will continue to grow and eventually will kill and ruin nature. This litter kills animals daily and also destroys our environment and the environment of innocent animals.


Our solution to this problem

Cleanliness is a problem found almost everywhere across the globe, but its effect on Egypt is like a deep wound: if it is not nurtured, it will leave a scar. Therefore we decided to create a website that will spread awareness and provide motivation for people to start volunteering by having volunteers win different prizes depending on how much trash they collected.
